About Pneumonia-AI

AI-Powered Pneumonia Detection For Faster Medical Analysis

PneumoniaAI is an intelligent medical platform designed to assist in the early detection of pneumonia from chest X-ray images.The dataset used in this project consists of pediatric chest X-ray images collected from children aged approximately 4 to 5 years old. The system is built using advanced deep learning models to ensure accurate and reliable prediction.It aims to support early diagnosis and improve healthcare assistance through AI-based analysis.
